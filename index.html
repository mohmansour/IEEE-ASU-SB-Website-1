About IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE, pronounced "I triple E") is a professional association. It was formed in 1963 from the amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Radio Engineers. Today, it is the world's largest association of technical professionals with more than 400,000 members in chapters around the world.
