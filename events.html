iCompete

iCompete is a technical competition that is designed to bring out your inner innovator.
and offers you the chance to reach your fullest technical potentials by offering a wide variety of track-based competitions designed specifically to challenge, test & evaluate your skills in all respects.

iCompete Focuses on helping you implement your technical knowledge into solving real life problems that are faced by industry leaders.
Facing these challenges will help you take the step you need to become a true engineer

iCompete can be considered as a selection phase for the internships program so, Challenges are determined and judged by our industrial partners.

Dates:

-Online registration(Mon 12/12/2016 - Sat 24/12/2016)
-Finalizing registration with payment (Sun 1/1/2017 - Mon 23/1/2017)
-List of Projects(Sat 14/1/2017)
-Online PST (Wed 25/1/2017 - Tue 31/1/2017)
iCompete is hosted by IEEE ASU SB & iHub targeting 2nd and also 3rd year students. iCompete consists of 8 different tracks.


These tracks are:


Energy Track:

In this track you will be working with power electronics and other power concepts to build solar pumps and battery systems


Communication Track:

In this track you will be dealing with wired and wireless communications in order to efficiently design communication systems for vehicle to vehicle commmunication or cellphone tower


Devices Track:

In this track you will be working on designing and developing electronic devices such as diodes and transistors


Electronics tracks:

In this track you will be applying your knowledge of analog and digital electronics in order to design a PCB to execute a required function efficiently


Embedded Track:

In this track you will be working with microcontrollers and/or microprocessors to design and make an embedded systems for autonomous cars or agriculture systems or industrial control systems


Software Track:

In this track you will use your programming skills with various programming languages to create web platforms and mobile applications that provide a certain service


Mechatronics Track:

In this track you will be asked to design a control system and to control mechanical parts for projects like autonomus cars,house safety system


Mechanical Track:

In this track you will focus on designing.in which you will be applying your knowledge of stress analysis,mechanical design,CAD tools in order to make a reliable design for the required tasks(to be announced)


Architecture Track:

In this track you will be working on the new design of ASU faculty of engineering in the new Capital

Registartion Fees

200 EGP (to be returned to you when you complete the competition till the end)

Prizes/awards

All teams will be evaluated by our industrial partners. And the winning team from each track will be rewarded with 3000 EGP.

Eligibility


1. Undergraduate student in an Egyptian University
2. This program mainly targets 2nd & 3rd year students
3. Have a passion to learn and willing to dedicate 20 hours per week for 8 weeks
